Homemade Pizza Recipe

Learn how to make a delicious homemade pizza from scratch!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add olive oil and warm water, then knead into a dough.
  3. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough and place on a baking sheet.
  5. Spread tomato sauce and add cheese and toppings.
  6. Bake at 450°F (230°C) for 15-20 minutes.

Did you know?

Pizza originated in Naples, Italy, in the late 18th century.
